Board of Directors

The people who show up, roll up their sleeves, and make Mill Creek a better place to live, work, and do business.

Executive Officers

Chamber Leadership

President
Greg Elwin
Real Estate Broker  ·  Greg Elwin Real Estate / Raintown Realty

"Our Chamber is unique due to its broad range of membership inclusivity. We feel that if your business does even a little bit of business in town, we want you to be involved. The mantra 'live, work and play' is so true here. It's important for all of us to share our talents and skills for the betterment of our community and our organization. We want everyone who's part of it to be involved."

Vice President
Marc Zimmerman
Owner  ·  4Z Design, Marketing & Printing

"Mill Creek stands out because it's truly community-focused. Other groups I've been part of leaned heavy on sales and transactions. Here, the goal is simple: make Mill Creek better. That focus changes everything — the energy, the collaboration, and the impact. I'm here because I care about my community. Serving on the Board lets me help shape events, strengthen relationships, and contribute to something bigger than my own business."

Secretary
Darshana Maru

"Mill Creek is a vibrant and diverse community that I am proud to be affiliated with. It is rewarding to contribute through volunteering and fostering community engagement with various events throughout Mill Creek and the Town Center. I am excited to be a part of our community growth."

Treasurer

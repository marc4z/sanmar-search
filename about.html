Our Mission

Rooted in Mill Creek. Built for Business.

The Mill Creek Chamber of Commerce provides an opportunity for local businesses to connect with city officials, local leaders, and the larger community. We advocate, we connect, and we create the conditions for businesses to grow.

"We invite you to join our outstanding business community and see how the Chamber can grow your business and build our community."

Led by Adri Boer, Interim Director, our team works every day to make Mill Creek a stronger, more connected place for everyone who lives and works here.

Community Impact

More Than a Chamber

Beyond business networking, the Chamber is woven into the fabric of Mill Creek life — from the biggest festival of the year to holiday traditions that bring the whole community together.

🎪

Mill Creek Festival

The Chamber organizes the annual Mill Creek Festival & Street Fair — 25 years of live music, local vendors, food, and community celebration. July 11 & 12, 2026.

🎶

Community Events

The Chamber sponsors beloved community traditions including the Summer Concert Series, Trunk or Treat, and the Christmas Tree Lighting — bringing Mill Creek together throughout the year.

🎓

Scholarships & Education

The Chamber awarded $12,000 in scholarships to local students and actively supports other scholastic efforts in the Northshore and Mill Creek community.

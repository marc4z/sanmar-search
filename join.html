Why It Works

Membership Benefits

🤝

Create Connections

There are clients, prospects, and partners out there who need your business. Join a prestigious local business group and connect with them — non-targeted advertising can't do what a strong network can.

🏘️

Get Involved

Ample opportunities to participate in community events, initiatives, and causes. Getting involved raises your business profile while giving back to the Mill Creek community you call home.

📣

Become Visible

Your business can only be great if people know about it. Membership provides advertising opportunities on the Chamber's website, mailing list, and at various functions and events throughout the year.

📈

Grow Your Business

Chamber membership generates exposure and increased word-of-mouth. For many local businesses, that means more feet in the door and the right contacts to improve your bottom line.

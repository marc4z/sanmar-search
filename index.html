About the Chamber

Rooted in Mill Creek. Built for Business.

The Mill Creek Chamber of Commerce connects businesses with city officials, local leaders, and the people who live and work here. We've been proudly advocating for local commerce and community vitality since 2000.

"We invite you to join our outstanding business community and see how the Chamber can grow your business and build our community."

Led by Adri Boer, Interim Director, our dedicated team works daily to create the connections that move Mill Creek forward.
